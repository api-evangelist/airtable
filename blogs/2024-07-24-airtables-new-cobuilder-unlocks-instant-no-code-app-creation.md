<h3 id="access-the-no-code-power-of-airtable-instantly"><strong>Access the no-code power of Airtable, instantly</strong></h3><p>AI is poised to drive a sea change in software as we know it today. On its own, AI can be an incredibly powerful tool for software development. When combined with no-code, it unlocks an entirely new era of application development &#x2013; enabling the people working on your most critical workflows to become capable app builders in <em>seconds</em> with the assistance of a smart AI cobuilder.</p><p>Airtable Cobuilder combines the vast knowledge of today&#x2019;s large language models (LLMs) with the accessibility of Airtable&#x2019;s no-code app platform to bring net-new ideas to life. It leverages the knowledge these models have across industries, use cases, and business concepts to immediately translate your desired outcome into a very detailed app&#x2014;that you can then customize and tweak with a simple click.&#xa0;</p><ul><li><strong>Effortless way to turn ideas into apps: </strong>Users can describe their idea to Airtable Cobuilder. Example: &#x201c;I&#x2019;m a director for the next Dune movie, and I need an app to manage my pre-production before filming begins in January 2025.&#x201d; This generates a preview of a custom Airtable application in seconds, allowing teams to reimagine legacy workflows and experiment with new ways of working.&#xa0;</li><li><strong>Leveraging the power of AI: </strong>The power of Airtable Cobuilder rests in its ability to leverage the latest generation of LLMs to build extremely personalized apps. When users provide additional details like the company they work for or project they&#x2019;re working on <em>(Dune)</em>, function <em>(Director)</em>, specific requirements they need <em>(lead up to January 2025)</em>, or describe the workflow they&#x2019;re trying to build <em>(manage film&#x2019;s pre-production)</em>, Cobuilder is able to generate a highly-specific and unique app &#x2013; one that leverages the knowledge base of LLMs.</li><li><strong>Cobuilding with AI: </strong>One size doesn&#x2019;t fit all when it comes to business apps. After initial generation, users have the ability to preview the app and make any needed adjustments to suit their exact needs. This is where no-code shines: instead of needing a developer to review, debug, and edit code to make changes, anyone can edit the app. You can focus less on building, and more on customizing, automating, and enabling the work that matters.&#xa0;</li><li><strong>Data privacy and security: </strong>Airtable protects the privacy and security of customers&apos; data. No customer data is used to train current or future LLMs.&#xa0;</li><li><strong>More to come: </strong>Instant, no-code app building with Cobuilder is just the beginning. We are building towards a world where Cobuilder allows you to make all kinds of app customizations and automations as you get deeper into the work: ask Cobuilder to add a new base populated with supplied content, generate insights at scale, build a timeline to track deadlines, and more.</li></ul><h3 id="describe-the-app-you-need-let-cobuilder-do-the-rest"><strong>Describe the app you need. Let Cobuilder do the rest.&#xa0;</strong></h3><p>While Airtable is designed to be easy to use, starting from scratch can feel daunting &#x2013; and new builders still sometimes struggle to uncover the platform&#x2019;s full power when they first start building applications.</p><p>Cobuilder makes it possible for anyone to go from an initial idea to the full first draft of an app without any effort. This not only accelerates time-to-value for each use, but also increases the number of useful apps that can be created within an organization. And, best of all, apps built with Cobuilder carry the same value of any app made with Airtable&#x2014;consistent, best-in-class interfaces for the end user, structured underlying data, and logic and automations.&#xa0;</p><p>Additional prompt examples include:</p><ul><li>&#x201c;I need to launch a women&#x2019;s skateboarding shoe in time for the Olympics.&#x201d;</li><li>&#x201c;Help me track renovations of our boutique hotels in Marrakesh.&#x201d;</li><li>&#x201c;I need to track the parts and production schedule for the Artemis Mission SLS rocket system.&#x201d;</li></ul><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img alt="Airtable&#x2019;s new Cobuilder unlocks instant no-code app creation" class="kg-image" height="1138" src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXd15fz8LmKKtgeGsEslKmsRTknhHafX8ZbOrMzuua8F4PaAWZJw6ESxWhoRZmXT8lSlmUy1INnDrwpebACLxsYuUo_OdbkUfMgB1NNlvhmMw99-YkXVHM1XYqNxgkHwGQK090A-5k3O5cXIOBy5BzL-OY-x?key=acc06vsCn6BrSRmW5DOsBw" width="1578" /></figure><p>During our beta, we saw widespread adoption amongst customers within the media, tech, financial services, and retail industries.
